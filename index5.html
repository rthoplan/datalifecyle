What is SQL?

Structured Query Language – a declarative standard for defining, manipulating, and querying data in relational databases. First created at IBM in the 1970s (originally SEQUEL), SQL underpins systems from MySQL and PostgreSQL to Snowflake and BigQuery.

Why use SQL in this module?

  • Lets you filter, aggregate, and join gigabyte-scale tables close to the storage layer.
  • Integrates seamlessly with R via RMySQL, DBI, odbc, and dbplyr.
  • Combine strong back-end queries with R-side modelling.
