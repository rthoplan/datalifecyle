An Interactive Guide to Data Sources

Click on any category to expand and see details.

Internal Data Sources

Data originating from within the organization.

Customer Data

Feedback forms, surveys, customer support logs, CRM systems

Employee Data

Payroll systems, performance evaluations, satisfaction surveys

Marketing Data

Website analytics, email marketing tools, social media insights

Operational Data

Sales transactions, customer service records, inventory logs

Production Data

Machine sensors, production output, defect rates, quality control

Financial Data

Budget reports, balance sheets, income statements, cash flows

External Data Sources

Data collected from outside the organization.

Public Data

Government portals, research journals, newspapers

Social Media Data

Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn posts, user-generated content

Web Scraping

Automated extraction of competitor pricing, reviews, product listings

IoT Data

Sensor data from wearables, smart home devices, industrial IoT systems

Open Data

Aggregated or high-level public datasets freely available (e.g., World Bank, UN, data.gov)
